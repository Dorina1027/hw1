RELATIONSHIPS:
==============
- STUDIOS (1) -----> (N) MOVIES
  A studio produces many movies; a movie belongs to one studio.

- MOVIES (1) -----> (N) ROLES
  A movie has many roles; a role belongs to one movie.

- ACTORS (1) -----> (N) ROLES
  An actor plays many roles; a role is played by one actor.

- AGENTS (1) -----> (N) ACTORS
  An agent represents many actors; an actor has one agent (optional).

MANY-TO-MANY RELATIONSHIP:
==========================
MOVIES <-----> ACTORS (via ROLES join table)
  An actor can appear in multiple movies.
  A movie can have multiple actors.
  The ROLES table resolves this many-to-many relationship and stores
  the character_name for each actor-movie combination.

ENTITIES:
=========
1. STUDIOS - Movie production studios
2. MOVIES - Films with title, year, rating
3. ACTORS - People who act in movies
4. AGENTS - Representatives for actors
5. ROLES - Join table linking actors to movies with character names
